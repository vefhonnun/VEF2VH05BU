# Website for an Event

### Goals:

* Students should be able to prepare and plan the creation of a promotional website.
* Students gather their own material and images to use in the final project.

### Preparation and Planning

1. Topic and content are _open choice_.
1. An event at a specific time
   - A promotional website for an event, service, or product
1. There should be an option to buy tickets or order a product.
1. Program/schedule or services that are offered.
1. It is a plus if there is some sales incentive on the website that customers can participate in.
   - Include a contact form for signing up to participate in a game/offer.

### Content Collection

> Write down what should appear on the website.

* Information about events, products, and/or services
* Sales incentive, game, or lottery/raffle
* Program/schedule, services, or product list offered (table)
* Order form or registration form
  * A short description of how interaction should work between user and website.
* Contact form - chat channel
  * _In the final project, you only design the form appearance - no programming._
* Information about the company behind the exhibition/event

#### Preparation Materials

1. Site structure - _"SiteMap"_
1. Diagram - _"WireFrame"_
1. Visual style - _Style tyle_
